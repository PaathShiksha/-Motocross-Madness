Motocross Game - (Motorcycle)

Game Rules:

  • The objective of the game is to avoid colliding with the obstacles (fireballs) and stay on the track.
  • Use the arrow keys to control the player's bike:
    • Up Arrow:- Accelerate
    • Down Arrow:- Brake
    • Left Arrow:- Turn left
    • Right Arrow:-Turn right
  • Colliding with obstacles or going off the track will end the game.
  • Your score increases as you progress. Try to achieve the highest score!
  • The game includes various types of enemies, including fireballs and human enemies, which you must avoid.
  • As you progress in the game, the difficulty level increases, making it more challenging.
